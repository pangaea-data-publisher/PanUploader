JubanyThumnailBuilder
=====================

The software JubanyThumnailBuilder downloads pictures from the Jubny webcam, creates thumbnails, and uploads thumbnails to the PANGAEA webserver.

Developer and contact Rainer Sieger. The software is provided as freeware without warranty by the Alfred Wegener Institute for Polar and Marine Research (AWI).
